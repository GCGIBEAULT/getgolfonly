About GolfSolo

GolfSolo is built for golfers who want a clean, fast, distraction‑free way to keep score. No accounts. No ads. No pop‑ups. No tracking. No upsells. Just golf.

Every round starts instantly. Every hole takes seconds. Your scorecard is always visible, always simple, always yours. GolfSolo is the digital pencil — nothing more, nothing less.

Whether you’re walking nine at sunset or grinding through 18 on the weekend, GolfSolo stays out of your way so you can breathe, swing, and play.

Features

  • Fast scoring — 5 seconds per hole
  • Offline — works with zero signal
  • No login, no data collection
  • Clean, minimalist design
  • Automatic totals and tracking
